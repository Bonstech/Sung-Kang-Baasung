About Our Foundation

Learn about our vision, mission, and the values that drive our work

Our Vision

To build an inclusive and empowered society where young people and vulnerable groups realize their full potential through education, skills development, and community support.

Our Mission

The Sung Kang Baasung Foundation exists to empower young people and communities by:

  • Promoting education through school visits, mentorship, and academic support.
  • Equipping youth with employable and entrepreneurial skills for self-reliance.
  • Advocating for the rights and inclusion of vulnerable groups.
  • Driving community-centered initiatives that inspire development, discipline, and leadership.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Compassion

We approach our work with empathy and understanding for those we serve.

Integrity

We maintain honesty and transparency in all our actions and relationships.

Innovation

We continuously seek creative solutions to address community challenges.

Excellence

We strive for the highest quality in all our programs and initiatives.

Our Story

The Sung Kang Baasung Foundation was established in 2010 with a simple yet powerful belief: every young person deserves the opportunity to realize their full potential, regardless of their background or circumstances.

What began as a small community initiative has grown into a comprehensive foundation that serves thousands of youth and vulnerable individuals across multiple regions. Our journey has been marked by milestones of growth, learning, and impactful partnerships.

Today, we continue to expand our reach and deepen our impact, always guided by our core mission of empowering young people and communities through education, skills development, and inclusive support.

Our Journey & Impact

Key milestones in our history of community transformation

2010

Foundation Established

Sung Kang Baasung Foundation was officially registered with a focus on youth education and empowerment.

2013

First Vocational Training Center

Opened our first skills development center, offering courses in entrepreneurship and employable skills.

2016

Community Advocacy Program

Launched our advocacy initiative for the rights and inclusion of vulnerable groups.

2020

Digital Learning Initiative

Adapted our programs for online delivery during the pandemic, reaching even more beneficiaries.

2023

10,000+ Lives Impacted

Celebrated impacting over 10,000 young people and community members through our various programs.
